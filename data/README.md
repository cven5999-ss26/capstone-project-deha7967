## Methodological information

1.  Description of methods used for collection/generation of data:Data were collected using structured household questionnaires administered through face-to-face interviews. The survey gathered information on household demographics, livelihoods, agriculture, livestock ownership, natural resource use, income sources, expenditures, and perceptions regarding park conservation.

2.  Methods for processing the data:Survey responses were collected using digital questionnaire through Kobo collect app and cleaned through validation checks. Missing values, inconsistencies, and duplicate entries were reviewed and corrected where possible. Variables were coded and organized for analysis.

3.  Instrument- or software-specific information needed to interpret the
    data: Structured household survey questionnaire, Kobotoolbox app
